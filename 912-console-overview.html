The Challenge
Running a company blind
Before this system, managing the finances of a diversified group of companies meant juggling spreadsheets, logging into multiple platforms, chasing staff for updates, and never having a single source of truth.
📊

No Real-Time Cash Visibility

Knowing how much money had come in this month required manually exporting from Zoho Books and cross-referencing expense records. There was no live profit number.

🗂️

Quotes Lost in the Pipeline

Quotes were created but no one tracked whether they were sent, accepted, or quietly expired. Revenue was leaking from the pipeline with no accountability.

💸

Unpaid Invoices Slipping Through

Chasing overdue payments required manually scanning the invoices list. No daily chase list meant clients were sometimes months late before anyone noticed.

👥

No Staff Performance Visibility

Management had no way to see how many quotes each team member was generating or what their conversion rate was. High performers and low performers looked the same.

🔀

Tool Sprawl

Zoho Books, spreadsheets, WhatsApp, email threads, and verbal updates. Critical business information lived in different places and was never consolidated.

⏱️

Admin Time Wasted

Hours every week were lost to manual data pulling, report building, and answering questions that a proper system would answer instantly and automatically.

The answer wasn't another SaaS tool. It was a system built exactly for how this business works.

The 912 Finance Console was engineered from scratch — wired directly into Zoho Books, tailored to the exact workflows of Waitara Holdings, and designed so that any team member can open it and immediately understand where the business stands.
